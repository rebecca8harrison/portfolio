Rebecca Harrison (BA, MSC) Portfolio

Rebecca Harrison is a freelance epidemiologist and data scientist and has 11 years of experience working in data and research in public health, with a particular focus on infectious disease, sexual and reproductive health, low and middle income countries and humanitarian crises.
I have a high level of technical capability in qualitative or quantitative data collection and analysis; analysis in STATA, SQL, R, Python; writing research protocols and report or manuscript writing and presentation for external and internal audiences

    Adventure Game

    In this project, I have created a game in python, where the player has to find their way out of a set rooms.

    Earthquake data analysis

    In this project, I have used seaborn, matplotlibs, pandas, numpy, pptx and other packages to explore and visualise data.

    Unsupervised learning

    In this app, I have used scikit-learn, seaborn, matplotlibs, pandas, numpy, and streamlit to classify countried into whether or not they are eligible for aid or not. I've compared hierarchical and k means clustering, and identified the most useful number of clusters
